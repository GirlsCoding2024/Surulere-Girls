BUILT WITH GREATNESS

At Surulere Girls Senior Secondary School, we believe in nurturing not just students, but future leaders. It was established in the year 1985. Our school is a beacon of excellence, where every girl is empowered to explore her full potential. With a strong emphasis on academic achievement and personal growth, we create an environment that inspires creativity, critical thinking, and resilience. Our dedicated and qualified teachers are passionate about guiding each student on her journey to success. They provide not only knowledge but also mentorship, encouraging us to dream big and pursue our aspirations with confidence. The supportive community fosters collaboration and friendship, ensuring that we uplift each other as we strive for greatness.

Surulere Girls Senior Secondary School is more than an educational institution; it is a launching pad for our ambitions. We are equipped with the skills and values necessary to face challenges head-on and to contribute positively to society. Together, we are building a brighter future, one filled with possibilities and achievements. It is a place where dreams are nurtured and where young women are empowered to reach their full potential. We are not just students,we are warriors who fight for our educational right,our future and our place in the world. Join us in this journey of growth, where every achievement is celebrated and every dream is within reach!




SCHOOL MISSION

Raising all round developed girls with competitive skills through improved teaching and leaning process,capacity building and modern instructional materials for enhanced performance

SCHOOL VISION

To raise all round developed girls with competitive skills for self reliance and societal development

SCHOOL PLEDGE

I pledge to uphold the values of excellence,integrity and perservance.
I commit to striving for academic success with diligence and dedication,always challenging myself to reach new heights of knowledge and understanding.

DRESS CODE

For our school dress code, I propose an outfit that embodies our school colors of wine and grey. This ensemble features a wine beret, a grey collar shirt, and a wine pleated skirt, complemented by grey socks and black shoes. To add a touch of formality, a wine tie can be worn. For Muslim students, a wine hijab would complete the look. Our school colors represent pride and unity, reflecting our commitment to excellence and fostering a strong community. Wearing these colors not only promotes school spirit but also creates a sense of belonging among students.
