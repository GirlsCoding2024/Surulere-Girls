DESCRIPTION OF S.G.S.S.S

It is painted yellow and brown with a blue roof, It is a three storey building. It is a well disciplined school. It gives a platform to do better in life. Our school is characterised by a supportive community, consistent and purposeful feedback, a safe learning environment, excellent resources and facilities, and a commitment to extra-curricular activities. It is a dynamic institution Our teachers have skills in communication, listening, collaboration, adaptability, empathy and patience. Our principal also plays a very significant role in the lives of students on a daily basis.
