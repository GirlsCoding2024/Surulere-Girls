MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL

MRS ADERIBIGBE FOLASHADE F.


Dear Students, Parents, and Staff,
Welcome to Surulere Girls Senior Secondary School. As we embark on another exciting academic year, I want to take a moment to express my gratitude for the incredible community we have built together. Our commitment to fostering a safe, inclusive, and engaging learning environment remains our top priority.
This year, we are focused on enhancing our educational programs, encouraging creativity, and promoting critical thinking skills. We believe that every student has the potential to excel, and we are dedicated to supporting each child on their individual journey.
I encourage parents to stay involved and participate in school activities. Your support and engagement make a significant difference in our students' success. Together, we can create a positive and inspiring atmosphere for all.
Thank you for being a part of the Surulere Girls Senior Secondary School family. Let's make this year one of growth, achievement, and lasting memories!

Warm regards,
Mrs Aderibigbe Folashade
